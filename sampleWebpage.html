Plank

Targets: Abdominals and core muscles

The plank is one of the simplest exercises that you can do – it’s simple in its appearance and the amount of steps involved in doing one.

Benefits

  • Strengthen Core
  • Improve Posture
  • Relieve Backpain

How to do a Plank

  1. Make sure your elbows are directly under your shoulders
  2. Line your wrists up with your elbows
  3. Push your body upwards, and keep your chin tight to your neck

The recommended time for beginners is 30 seconds, but if you can’t do that, you can consider anything less to be good practice – or switch to a forearm plank, where you rest your weight on your forearms instead of your palms, allowing you to hold the position for much longer.
